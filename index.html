The Baltimore Bartenders’ Guild is a collection of Baltimore local like-minded bar professionals with one goal, to raise the bar. To bring a new level of classic and contemporary cocktails to the city of Baltimore, while providing guests with the best quality of service.

    BBG's 4th Annual "Ryes Up Against Cystic Fibrosis" Charitable Gala

    Location: B&O American Brasserie

    The Baltimore Bartenders' Guild will be hosting our 4th Annual Charitable Gala, "Ryes Up Against Cystic Fibrosis." One night only will the BBG's craftiest bartenders be in one location shaking and stirring delicious libations for your enjoyment. Also some of Baltimore finest restaurants will be serving up tasty morsels to satisfy your palate. Live jazz, a silent auction, local beer by Union Craft Brew, vino and local gin for you non rye spirit drinkers will be available. Get your tickets today!!!
